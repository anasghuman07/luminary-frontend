Meet the team

Luminary began on a rain-soaked ridge in Patagonia. A group of friends, lost and laughing, decided the world needed better guides — ones built on honesty, depth, and genuine local knowledge.

Since 2021, we've helped over 180,000 explorers find their way — from the Dolomites to the alleys of Marrakech. Every route has been walked, paddled, or climbed by a real person who cares about sharing it honestly.

Why we exist

The story behind Luminary

Travel has become too transactional. Bucket lists, fast check-ins, filtered photos — we believe in slow discovery. In the detour that changes everything. In conversations with strangers who become lifelong friends.

Luminary was built to help you find those moments. Our platform connects curious travellers with local experts, hand-curated routes, and a community that values depth over breadth.

Since 2021, we've helped over 180,000 explorers find their way — from the peaks of the Dolomites to the alleys of Marrakech. Every route on our platform has been walked, paddled, or climbed by a real human being who cares about sharing it honestly.
